Rick and Morty

Rick and Morty is a popular American animated sci-fi sitcom created by Justin Roiland and Dan Harmon for Cartoon Network’s late-night Adult Swim block. The show centers on the chaotic escapades of Rick Sanchez, a jaded, eccentric scientist, and his anxious but kind-hearted grandson Morty Smith. Their adventures blend ordinary family life with wild, interdimensional journeys across countless universes, often involving space travel through portals or Rick’s spaceship. The show contrasts everyday family issues with Rick’s dark, reckless behavior.

Originally, Roiland voiced both main characters until he was replaced in Season 7 by Ian Cardoni and Harry Belden. Other key cast members include Chris Parnell (Jerry), Spencer Grammer (Summer), and Sarah Chalke (Beth). The show began as a parody of Back to the Future, created by Roiland for Channel 101, a short film festival co-founded by Harmon.

A major renewal deal secured 70 episodes, extending the show to at least ten seasons, and by 2024, it was confirmed through Season 12. In 2023, Adult Swim severed ties with Roiland due to domestic abuse allegations and recast his roles. Season 7 premiered on October 15, 2023, and Season 8 followed on May 25, 2025.

Since its premiere, Rick and Morty has earned widespread praise for its clever writing, originality, and humor. It has won two Primetime Emmy Awards (2018 and 2020) and two Annie Awards. The series has frequently ranked as the top comedy among viewers aged 18 to 24 and has evolved into a lucrative franchise worth hundreds of millions in merchandise and media.
